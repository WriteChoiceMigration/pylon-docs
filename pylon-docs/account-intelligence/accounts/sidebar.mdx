## Customization

Customize the ordering and visibility of fields within the account sidebar from the [Account Sidebar](https://app.usepylon.com/settings/account-sidebar) settings page.

Hovering over a particular field will allow you to hide it entirely from the sidebar and dragging fields or will allow you to reorder them.
