## Language Selection

Inside your knowledge base settings, select the languages you want your knowledge base available in.

<Frame>
<img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="high" height="859" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/ff3a7da9.png" width="654"/>
</Frame>

When customers visit your knowledge base, they'll be able to view content in your published languages.

<Frame>
<img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="841" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/0a096376.png" width="1117"/>
</Frame>

## AI Translation

Pylon can help you write one copy of your articles in your native language and have AI keep copies in other languages up-to-date automatically.

<Frame>
<img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="1091" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/253e3810.png" width="1719"/>
</Frame>
