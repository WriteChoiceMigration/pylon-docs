Pylon is a pretty powerful platform with lots of customization to make your day-to-day productive and fun. Here are some of the things you'll want to get setup or learn about to help you get the most value out of Pylon.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

All keyboard shortcuts can be found in-app by choosing "Keyboard Shortcuts" from your profile menu at the bottom left corner of the screen.

## General Navigation

To quickly navigate around Pylon, view recently visited pages, or search for specific customer issues, knowledge base articles, accounts, etc use [Command Search](/pylon-docs/platform/command-search) by hitting `Cmd-K` or `Ctrl-K` from anywhere in Pylon.

## Dark Mode

Toggle dark mode from your [Profile](https://app.usepylon.com/settings/profile) page in Settings or by typing `Cmd-k` and then `Dark Mode`.

## Notification Preferences

Under the [Notifications](https://app.usepylon.com/settings/notifications) page, customize which notifications you'd like to receive and where you'd like to receive them. You can also access them from both the profile and settings menus in the bottom left corner of your screen.

In-app notifications will show up under your notifications tray, accessible via. the Bell icon on the top left.

## Signatures

If you'll be sending emails from Pylon, consider setting up an email signature on your [Profile](https://app.usepylon.com/settings/profile) page. The signature will get automatically appended in the editor when you're replying to emails.
