## Additional Setup

## Set Bot Name

When Pylon is enabled in a customer channel, the customer will see that `@Pylon was added to the channel`. You can rename the bot so that instead it shows `@[YourCompany Support] was added to the channel` instead.

1. In Slack, click `Add Apps` in the sidebar

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="439" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/1d25ae29.png" width="504"/>
    </Frame>

2. Find and click `Pylon`

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="439" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/27429aae.png" width="706"/>
    </Frame>

3. Click the `About` tab

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="400" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/dbc3579b.png" width="530"/>
    </Frame>

4. Click `Configuration`

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="416" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/50b2625a.png" width="680"/>
    </Frame>

5. Scroll down to the section called `Bot User` and click `Edit`

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="169" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/249875ae.png" width="600"/>
    </Frame>

6. Enter the name of your company plus the work “Support” and hit `Save Changes`

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="301" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/b6c87919.png" width="571"/>
    </Frame>
