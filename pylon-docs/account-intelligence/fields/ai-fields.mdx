AI fields allow you to rollup qualitative insights into structured fields and provide **explainability** for account signals. AI fields can be any data type and can be prompted to look through particular data, most commonly [AI Context](/pylon-docs/account-intelligence/notebooks/blocks#ai-context) in [Notebooks](/pylon-docs/account-intelligence/notebooks). AI fields can draw from context in any communication source, call recordings, CRM data fields, and much more.

<Info>
Fields are fully customizable based on your use case!
</Info>

### Example: Low Sentiment

For example, rollup a "Low Sentiment" field on accounts into a True/False value to use in calculations, and also surface the reason the customer has low sentiment.

Use AI fields to surface upsell signals into a multiselect [Custom Fields](/pylon-docs/platform/custom-fields) based on a prompt and set of options.

It interprets and structures the data into a field that can now be filtered on and used in views, triggers, etc.
