### Custom Activity Types

On the [Custom Activity Types](https://app.usepylon.com/settings/custom-activities) page in settings, create types of activity:

* Sync in custom signals from your own product
* Sync in signals from other systems

### Account Activity

Using the [activity API](/pylon-docs/developer/api/api-reference/accounts/activities), write in custom events that will show up in the account activity timeline and be accessible from AI features like [Ask AI](/pylon-docs/platform/ask-ai) and be available in the account  blocks. Account activities can also be aggregated on the account level with [Formulas](/pylon-docs/account-intelligence/fields/formulas)!
