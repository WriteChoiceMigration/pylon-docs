### Section Header

A header to help organize the content in your notebook. These show up in the table of contents in your sidebar for quick navigation.

### AI Context

A powerful section where you can extract details relevant to your business across all the interactions of an account, including communication from all sources (email, chat, Slack, etc), [Call Recording](/pylon-docs/integrations/call-recording), and external or custom data sources like your [CRM](/pylon-docs/integrations/crm), [Custom Activities](/pylon-docs/account-intelligence/activities/custom-activities), or API.

Generated context will come with citations to where the AI context came from. You have full control to customize prompts to extract the information useful to your business!
