## Setup

Click into the App Directory from your Pylon dashboard and then click on the Microsoft Teams integration to view setup instructions.

## Usage

Pylonâ€™s Microsoft Teams integration allows you to view, respond to, and track Microsoft Teams messages from your customer without using Teams yourself.

Messages from customer channels in Microsoft Teams are automatically tracked in Pylon as well as being mirrored into Slack.

### Receiving messages

1. Messages sent by your customer in the Teams channel are visible in your mirrored Slack channel, where any Slack channel members can see them regardless of whether they are in the Teams channel.

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="142" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/5c2c6c3f.png" width="520"/>
    </Frame>

2. Customer issues from Teams are visible in the Kanban board alongside with your other issues.

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="302" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/965a5c07.png" width="626"/>
    </Frame>

3. Messages are also bundled and visible in your internal threads channel along with other messages.

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="298" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/dc1019ad.png" width="1136"/>

### Sending messages

1. Messages and reactions on customer messages sent in your Slack channel are mirrored to Teams.

    <Frame>
    <img alt="" class="block" data-testid="zoom-image" fetchpriority="low" height="882" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 400px, 768px" src="/images/be12ce30.png" width="2210"/>
    </Frame>

2. Responding to customer issues from an Issue in Pylon sends the reply directly into Teams.
