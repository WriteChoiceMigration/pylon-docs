## Customization

Customize the ordering of modules the order and visibility of fields within the issue sidebar from the [Issue Sidebar](https://app.usepylon.com/settings/issue-sidebar) settings page.

Hovering over a particular field will allow you to hide it entirely from the sidebar and dragging fields or modules will allow you to reorder them.
